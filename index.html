Breeds

So you're looking for a cat, but dont know what breeds there are and how they'd act. You need to see what they look like, how they act, where to find them, etc. So let's start looking! The 3 best breeds will be shown here!

Ragdoll

The Ragdoll is a cat breed with beautiful fur and much known for its amazing blue eyes. Their fur is very soft, they can also be very affectionate. These cats can be a good company for children and familys. Overall very friendly, loving and gentle with their owner.

Scottish Fold

The Scottish Fold is a breed of domesstic cat which is very gentle, quiet, very loving and affectionate. Mostly known for the way their ears "fold", which gives the cat an "owl-like" appearance. They can be easy going and overall very friendly, best for household pets. Also adore human attention.

Persian Longhair

The Persian Longhair is a cat breed that is mostly known for its adorable face amd short muzzle. One of the cutest cat breeds, even though all cats are cute right? Can be a varaity of colors and very beautiful. Mostly sweet and well known for how adorable they look.

Tips All Cat Owners Should Know!!

Purrrr..

When a cat purs, it can be a sign of affection towards you. In most cases, it has a meaning where the cat is showing that they trust you, they glad you're their owner and are happy to see you. For example, when you get home from a hard day at work, your cat is most likely to rub their head on your leg. They are checking you came back alive from hunting, and are happy to see you! ( cats think you go out to hunting for food, yeah its a bit funny. )

Belly Show, Belly Hide

Cats can be very protective over their bellys. It has a cats vital organs and can be very sensitive for a human to touch, but when a cat shows their belly to you, you know you have one over their trust. Cats that show their belly near you can mean that they trust being around you, they trust you protect them. In most cases, cats doing this does not mean they want a belly rub, each cat can have their own prefence. ( When I try to touch my cats belly, he bites me. )

Hidings

In the cases a cat hides, it could mean he feels danger or is sick. This could be because of an infection or a feeling the cat dislikes. Ive come to this conclusion because of a personal expierence with my own cat, he had been hiding for a while when I realized he had an infection. He was peeing blood and was slowly dying, I was able to notice and brought him to the vet immidently.
